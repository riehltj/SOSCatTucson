a few meows about us

SOS Cat Rescue AZ, Inc is a foster-based, volunteer-run, no kill, non-profit animal rescue group based out of Tucson AZ. The organization is dedicated to saving orphan kittens and senior cats from shelters across Arizona and giving them a second chance at a happily ever after.

Volunteer your time

Have you thought about ways you can help animals in need but are not ready to adopt? Why not give fostering a try then. You get to enjoy a wonderful pet without the financial obligations of adopting and who knows, you may end up adopting him/her. Many foster families do.

Fostering is a temporary commitment and it means you provide the safety and love of your home and your family and we provide all the medical care if needed.

Fill some bellies

We realize you might not be able to adopt one of our cats but would like to help them out. You can become a sponsor for just a few dollars per month. Your donation helps to pay for the basic needs of each cat for the duration of his or her stay with us. You can follow their stories via our Facebook page SOS.catrescueAZ where we will give updates with current photos and stories.
